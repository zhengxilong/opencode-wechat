# Code of Conduct

[English](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [简体中文](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-CN.md)

## Our Commitment

We want `opencode-wechat` to be a welcoming, respectful project for contributors and users of different backgrounds, experience levels, and communication styles.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements with curiosity.
- Focus feedback on ideas, implementation, and impact.
- Share context clearly when reporting issues or proposing changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, or discussions

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, open a private maintainer contact path before the public launch, or document a preferred reporting address here once the repository is published.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation when necessary to keep the project safe and productive.
